The Coded by Kids Mission

Coded by Kids is committed to providing
tech education opportunities, tech industry
exposure and mentorship to inner city
children who lack access to these
opportunities. By providing our children
with free, engaging, challenging and
creative education programs we are able
to help them both overcome the
socioeconomic challenges they face and
gain access to opportunities in tech
related fields.
